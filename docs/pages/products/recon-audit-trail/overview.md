# Product Overview

Recon Audit Trail provides an end-to-end approach to tracking Salesforce data changes. Built natively on the platform, it captures up to 300 fields per object, records who made every change, and stores the before-and-after values alongside timestamps. Automated backup policies archive history files to Salesforce Files or Google Drive, while native list views, reports, and dashboards make the history searchable without leaving Salesforce. Critical fields can be marked for permanent retention so their audit trail is always accessible, even after archival jobs run.

## Key Outcomes

- **Comprehensive Coverage** — Monitor every important object with scale beyond the standard Field History Tracking limits.
- **Retention You Control** — Schedule backups and decide how long to keep history data in Salesforce versus external storage.
- **Actionable Visibility** — Deliver dashboards and reports that surface change patterns for admins, auditors, and compliance teams.
- **Native Administration** — Configure, review, and analyze change history using the Salesforce tools your teams already know.

## Architecture Highlights

- Native Salesforce package that respects org security and sharing.
- Automated backup engine with configurable cadence and storage targets.
- Searchable archives delivered through Salesforce list views and custom dashboards.
- Permanent retention flag to keep mission-critical field history immediately available.

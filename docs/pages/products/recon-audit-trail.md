# Recon Audit Trail

Recon Audit Trail delivers comprehensive change tracking for Salesforce data while keeping everything inside the core platform. It extends native Field History Tracking with high-capacity capture, automated backups, and accessible analytics so compliance teams can see who touched what data and when—without exporting to external tools.
